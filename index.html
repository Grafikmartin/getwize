Safety through Construction

Redefining software safety and shaping the market.

To enable all interested providers to develop certifiable, safety-critical software systems using a freely available platform.

Open market access for safety-critical applications will fundamentally simplify product development for all interested providers. This will give providers a significant competitive advantage and lead to shorter project cycles, higher quality, and substantial cost reductions for users.

Market Access Democratization of the development of systems for functional safety.
Cost Reduction Modular, open, and scalable approaches make software development significantly more cost-effective.
Faster Time
to Market Significant reduction of time-to-market for safety-critical applications.

Core Benefits

Currently, the development of safety-critical products—especially software—is complex and requires significant effort. This results in lengthy development, approval, and certification processes. The market is effectively closed to new entrants, as they lack the extensive expert knowledge required and have no practical way to build it up.
